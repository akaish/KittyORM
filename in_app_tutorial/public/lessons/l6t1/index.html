Use KittyORM in your projects and leave your feedback!

It is very simple to make KittyORM even greater. Just use it in your projects and leave your feedback. Easy as a pie:

  1. Visit KittyORM page located at https://akaish.github.io/KittyORMPages/ or KittyORM GitHub repository located at https://github.com/akaish/KittyORM to acquire instructions on configuring your project in order to use KittyORM.

  2. Implement your business logic without messing with SQL and a lot of boiler plate code for database operations.

  3. Leave your feedback, feature requests, questions or bug reports at Issues section of KittyORM project GitHub repository or send it to kittyorm@gmail.com with subject contains !feedback, !featurerequest, !question or !bugreport (optional but it would be very nice).
